\# Group Policy Configuration



\## Objective

To configure and enforce security policies across the domain using Group Policy in Active Directory.



\## Environment

\- Domain: DomainExpansion.com

\- Tool: Group Policy Management Console (GPMC)



\## Steps Performed

\- Accessed the Group Policy Management Console

\- Reviewed and modified account policies

\- Configured password policy settings such as complexity and length

\- Linked the Group Policy to the domain

\- Group Policy enables centralized management of security settings

\- Account policies are critical for enforcing password and lockout rules

\- Linking GPOs ensures policies are applied across the domain
