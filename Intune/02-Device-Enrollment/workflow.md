\# Microsoft Intune - Device Enrollment



\## Objective

To demonstrate device enrollment and registration in Microsoft Intune, connected with Microsoft Entra ID.



\## Environment

\- Platform: Microsoft Intune

\- Domain: DomainExpansion874.onmicrosoft.com

\- Integration: Connected with Microsoft Entra ID



\## Steps Performed

\- Navigated to Devices section in Intune

\- Verified enrolled devices

\- Checked device details including ownership, compliance, and join type
